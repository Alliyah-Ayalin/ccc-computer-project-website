MATHALINO

On February 26, 2022, Our Lady of Peace School (OLPS) hosted the MATHALINO: Battle of the Wizards 2022 with the theme, "Youth Empowerment: Unleashing Students' Critical Thinking and Problem-solving Skills in the New Normal through Digital Citizenship". All participants showcased their exemplary math skills as they compete with the other participants from the different DACSA schools.Cainta Catholic College's participant, Xej Ashton De Jesus of Grade 10- St. Aloysius Gonzaga, successfully bagged 3rd place in the said contest.
